Founder

Meet the Mind Behind Ikrossis Learning Solutions

Shahnaz A

Founder & Behavioral Transformation Strategist

PsychologistNeuroscience ResearcherLeadership FacilitatorNLP Practitioner

“Helping people think better, communicate better, and lead better.”

Not all workplace challenges are skill problems. Many are human behavior problems.

As a psychologist, neuroscience researcher, leadership facilitator, and behavioral transformation specialist, Shahnaz helps individuals and organizations understand the science behind human behavior and translate it into practical workplace success.

Her work brings together psychology, neuroscience, emotional intelligence, NLP, and experiential learning to create powerful learning experiences that move beyond awareness and drive meaningful behavioral change.

Over the years, she has worked with professionals across industries, helping leaders strengthen influence, teams improve collaboration, and organizations build cultures rooted in communication, accountability, and growth.

What makes her approach distinctive is the ability to simplify complex human behavior into practical, relatable, and actionable insights that participants can immediately apply in their professional and personal lives.

In Her Own Words

“I have always been fascinated by one question: Why do people know what to do, yet struggle to do it consistently?”

The answer led me into the worlds of psychology, neuroscience, emotional intelligence, and behavioral science.

The more I studied human behavior, the more I realized that lasting change does not happen through information alone. It happens through awareness, reflection, experience, and intentional practice.

Today, my mission is simple: To help individuals and organizations understand themselves better so they can communicate more effectively, lead more consciously, and perform at their highest potential.
